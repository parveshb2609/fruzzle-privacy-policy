This Privacy Policy explains how Fruzzle Arcade ("the app", "we", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile game available on Google Play.

1. Information We Collect

Fruzzle Arcade does not directly collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the third-party services integrated into the app may collect certain data automatically:

  • Device or Advertising ID – used by Google AdMob to serve and measure ads.
  • App interactions – how you interact with the app and ads, used for analytics and advertising.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics – collected by Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and fix bugs.

2. How We Use This Information

  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To analyse app performance and fix crashes via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • To improve the overall gameplay experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. The data described above is shared only with Google LLC for the purposes of advertising and analytics:

  • Google AdMob – ad serving and measurement.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – crash reporting and diagnostics.

Google's privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Stored Locally

Fruzzle Arcade stores the following data only on your device (never uploaded to any server):

  • Your best scores and game progress.
  • Your selected theme and display preferences.

This data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

5. Advertising

Fruzzle Arcade displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to show personalised ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time by resetting your Advertising ID in your device settings under Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID.

6. Children's Privacy

Fruzzle Arcade is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain your personal data on our servers. Crash logs and diagnostics collected by Firebase are retained according to Google's data retention policies. Local game data is retained on your device until the app is uninstalled.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Since we do not store personal data on our servers, most requests can be fulfilled by uninstalling the app and resetting your Advertising ID. For any other requests, please contact us at parvesh.bhandari2609@gmail.com.
